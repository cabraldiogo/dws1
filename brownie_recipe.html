Recipe of the Week

Dark Chocolate Brownie

Ingredients

  • 1 2/3 cups of Dark Chocolate Morsels, divided
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/3 cup butter, cut into pieces
  • 2 tablespoons water
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 3/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
